About me

I received the B.S. degree from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, in 2017, and the M.S. degree from ShanghaiTech University, in 2020. I am currently a 2nd. year Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Tinne Tuytelaars at KU Leuven, Belgium.
